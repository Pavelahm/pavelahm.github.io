Weekly Reflection 5

a. Text Mining vs Close Reading

Text mining helps identify patterns in large datasets that are difficult to detect through close reading. It can reveal trends, sentiment, and relationships across thousands of documents. However, it may miss context, tone, and deeper meanings that human interpretation can capture.

b. NLP and LLM in Research

NLP and LLM can help in analyzing large text datasets, summarizing literature, and generating insights. For example, they can classify policy documents or extract themes from survey responses. They can also assist in writing and coding tasks.

c. Limitations and Solutions

Limitations include bias, lack of transparency, and potential errors. LLMs may generate incorrect or misleading information. These issues can be reduced by validating outputs, combining methods, and using human review.

d. AI for Research Guide

  • Always verify AI-generated results
  • Use AI as a support tool, not a replacement
  • Be aware of bias in datasets
  • Ensure ethical use of data
  • Document methods clearly
